Bio

Dr. Greg Warner graduated in medicine from Cambridge University and then Kings College London. He trained initially as a GP and became a partner in a practice in Romsey, Hampshire. His career expanded into teaching through Southampton University and then Sports Medicine, becoming a GP with a Special Interest in Musculo-Skeletal Medicine. He also headed up a number of Medicines Management initiatives, concentrating on safe prescribing. He has a keen interest in Risk Management and Patient Safety and this underlies his motivation in the Medico-Legal field. He continues to train GPs and teaches on the subject of “How to avoid mistakes as a GP”.
